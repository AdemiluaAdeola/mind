About Background Tab Behavior

Modern browsers throttle JavaScript timers in inactive tabs to save battery and improve performance. Our implementation:

  • Detects when the tab becomes inactive and adjusts the countdown accordingly
  • Uses precise timing to ensure the page reloads after exactly 30 seconds of active time
  • Provides visual feedback about the tab's active/inactive status
  • Compensates for browser throttling by calculating elapsed time when the tab becomes active again
